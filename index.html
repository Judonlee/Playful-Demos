Playful Perceptive Demos

Demo 1 : Patient Story Teller

Microphone input to demonstrate different types of sound (voice activity detection).

  • A looped piece of background sound is played while the recorded audio speaks over the top.
  • When a human voice is heard from the Microphone, the recorded audio finishes its sentence and waits a 5 seconds before continuing.
  • The background sound keeps looping throughout.

Ian provided background sounds from Derek Tangye documentary and even part of the transcript to make it quick and easy.

Demo 2 : Gather Around

A story is being told and is interupted ever time the sensors detect movement and faces.

  • Face entering and leaving
  • Face close and far from the camera
  • Lots of movement while a face detected
  • Face tilted to one side

Demo 3 : Setting the Pace

A story is being told and the light levels affect its (PBR) speed and its warmth (environment). A face being detected also changes the voice through Hi and Low pass filters.

  • Darker is slower. Lighter is faster. [PBR]
  • [WIP] Darker is warmer voice. Lighter is crisper voice. [Hi Pass]
  • Face not detected affects voice. [Convolution: max]
  • Face distance affects voice. [Convolution: scales to zero when close]
  • [WIP] Face tilt angle affects voice. [Low Pass]
